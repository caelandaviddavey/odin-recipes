Penne Pasta with Tomato Sauce

This is a recipe describing how to make my favourite dish - penne pasta with tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • De Cecco penne pasta
  • San Marzano canned tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Add 200ml of olive oil to a sauce pan over medium heat.
  2. Grate 1 clove of garlic and add to the sauce pan; cook until it yellows.
  3. Add 1 can of San Marzano canned tomatoes to the sauce pan.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste; let the sauce reduce until desired consistency.
  5. Boil water; once boiling add penne pasta and cook for 3 minutes less than the package recommends.
  6. Take out the pasta and add it to the sauce to finish cooking; selectively add pasta water to the tomato sauce.
  7. Taste noodle; remove from heat once the pasta is cooked to your liking.
